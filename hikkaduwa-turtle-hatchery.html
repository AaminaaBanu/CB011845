Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery

Hikkaduwa turtle hatchery is located 2km North to Hikkaduwa. The hatchery shows a good display of turtle eggs and also how it develops in to turtles in time. They also provide you with letting baby turtles in to the ocean for a additional fee paid.

The best time to visit the turtle hatchery is mostly March and April, but it is not bad either when you visit during different other times

  2. Boat riding
  3. Snorkelling and Cuba diving
