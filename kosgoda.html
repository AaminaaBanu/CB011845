KOSGODA TURTLE HATCHERY

Kosgoda sea turtle hatchery is about 9km from Bentota railway station. This sea turtle hatchery is located in the coast of Indian oceans in Kosgoda. Tourists are provided with proper guide on protecting and process of hatching of sea turtles.â€‹

About us

  • All 5 species of marine sea turtle that are found in Sri Lanka can be seen here.
  • You can gain knowledge on how sick and adult turtles are being nursed.
  • You can learn how baby turtles and turtle eggs are protected.
  • You can feed turtles and carry them in your hands.
  • During the season release the baby turtles back to ocean when they are ready to go back to the ocean.
